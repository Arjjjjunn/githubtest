About us

Founded with a passion for exploration and a commitment to exceptional service Memoriva Travel Agency has been helping travelers embark on transformative journeys since 2012. Our mission is simple: to provide personalized travel experiences that exceed expectations and leave a lasting impression

At Memoriva Travel Agency, we pride ourselves on our expertise and specialization in crafting tailor-made travel experiences that cater to the unique desires and interests of our clients. With years of industry experience and a deep passion for exploration, our team of seasoned travel experts possesses an intimate knowledge of a wide range of destinations, from iconic landmarks to hidden gems off the beaten path. Whether you're dreaming of sipping cocktails on a pristine Caribbean beach embarking on a thrilling safari adventure in the heart of Africa, or exploring the rich history and culture of ancient cities, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.
